Money Transfer

I worked as a UX/UI Designer and Motion Designer in the product team which had two product managers helping them develop the money transfer service as well as refining mobile top-ups flows and payments. Additionally, I took the lead in moving the design system from InVision into Figma, curating the grey color palette and font sizes, incorporating the updated iconography library and new components for actual and new services.

Thanks to Rebtel’s partnership with Ria Money Transfer, arises the possibility to offer Rebtel’s money transfer service to more than 120 countries across the globe through bank transfer, mobile wallet and cash pickup.


As a designer I worked very closed together with the Product Manager, the backend and frontend teams. I designed the entire screens flow of the Money Transfer service for Rebtel’s App, for both iOS and Android.
